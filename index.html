Dear MIT High School Students,

We're excited to launch our Club Stride CS Program Recruitment Challenge and invite you to participate! This is your opportunity to help grow our community while earning rewards for your recruitment efforts.

About the Club Stride CS Program

The new Computer Science program builds on Club Stride's successful model of youth empowerment through education and real-world experience. Similar to our 6-week Health Amplified internship, the CS program offers:

  • Hands-on tech skills that prepare you for future education and careers
  • Project-based learning with community partners
  • Networking opportunities with tech industry professionals
  • Collaborative problem-solving that addresses real needs in Solano County

The Challenge

As MIT High School students who participate in Club Stride programs, you're uniquely positioned to share your experience with other students who could benefit from our new CS program. Your personal stories and insights are our most powerful recruitment tools!

How It Works

As shown in the flyer, there are two ways to earn rewards:

Tier 1: Achievement Rewards

  • 1st Place: $300 for the student with the most completed enrollments
  • 2nd Place: $100 for the runner-up
  • 3rd Place: $50 for third place

Tier 2: Participation Rewards

Any student who gets 10+ people to attend orientation will be entered into a $250 raffle.

Remember the key difference:

  • Enrollments are participants who complete the full CS program onboarding process
  • Sign-ups are people who attend the CS program orientation session

Getting Started

  1. Pick up your CS program recruitment materials from the Club Stride office
  2. Share your MIT High School and Club Stride experience with potential participants
  3. Highlight the benefits of the new CS program and how it complements their education
  4. Invite them to attend a CS program orientation session
  5. Help guide them through the onboarding process
  6. Track your recruitment progress through our online portal

Important Timeline

  • April-May 2025: Weekly recruitment updates will be provided
  • May 5, 2025: Final submission deadline for all recruitments
  • May 12, 2025: Winners announced during Week 1 orientation

Tips for Success

  • Focus on how Club Stride programs have enhanced your MIT High School experience
  • Share specific examples of projects and skills you've developed
  • Emphasize how the CS program prepares students for future education and career opportunities
  • Highlight the collaborative, supportive community at Club Stride
  • Follow up with potential participants to encourage them to complete enrollment

This is a great opportunity to develop your leadership and communication skills while helping to build our CS program community. Your unique perspective as a current student is invaluable in attracting new participants.
